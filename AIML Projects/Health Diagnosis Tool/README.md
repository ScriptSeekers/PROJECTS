# 🧬 BIO-ENGINE | Advanced Health Matrix

**BIO-ENGINE** is a high-end, standalone health diagnostic assistant designed to analyze patient vitals and symptoms through an autonomous intelligence matrix. Built with a premium glassmorphic aesthetic, it provides heuristic-based diagnostic mapping across 20+ medical conditions.

## ✨ Core Features

*   **Advanced Bio-Telemetry Input**: Capture comprehensive patient data including Pulse Rate (BPM), Blood Pressure (Systolic/Diastolic), BMI, and Body Temperature.
*   **Heuristic Logic Engine**: Real-time diagnostic matching against a database of 20+ clinical conditions including Cardiovascular, Respiratory, and Endocrine disorders.
*   **Risk Stratification**: Automatic classification of health risks (Low/Medium/High) with visual color-coded badges.
*   **Interactive Medical Insights**: Dynamic disease cards containing deep-dives into symptomatic patterns, etiology (causes), prevention, and treatments.
*   **Privacy-First Architecture**: 100% client-side processing. Clinical data never leaves the browser and is never stored in external databases.

## 🚀 Deployment

The Bio-Engine is a zero-dependency standalone application.
1. Download the repository.
2. Open `HDA.html` in any modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, Brave, etc.).

## 🏥 Clinical Coverage

The engine currently maps patterns for:
- **Cardiovascular**: Hypertension, Tachycardia, Bradycardia, CAD.
- **Respiratory**: Asthma, Pneumonia, Influenza, COVID-19.
- **Metabolic/Endocrine**: Diabetes Mellitus, Hypo/Hyperthyroidism, Obesity.
- **General Wellness**: Dehydration, Anemia, Migraine, Gastroenteritis.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

**BIO-ENGINE** uses heuristic patterns for educational purposes only. It is **NOT** a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.
